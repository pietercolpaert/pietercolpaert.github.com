E-government

Open Data Portals

  • I have started the DataTank, which is data portal software for local governments. It complies to the European DCAT-AP standard. Furthermore, it gets the best out of your dataset by lifting it as high as possible on the scale of the 5 stars of Linked Open Data.
  • In order to sketch the roadmap of The DataTank, I have written a paper for the MeTTeG conference on the 5 priorities for data portals.
  • I have written an article on how to quantify the semantic interoperability of datasets, published in the October 2014 edition of Computer.

  • I'm working on DCAT tools for Belgium as part of the dcat.be working group: a way to link together all Belgian data portals
